Background

Andre specialises in photographing surfers and bodyboarders in action from the shoreline at
Cape Paterson.
Living and surfing in the area for most of his life,
Andre's local knowledge and experience show in
his photographs.
Now unable to surf after a recent surfing accident, focuses on capturing and sharing the stoke with
friends old and new.

Equipment & Technique

  • Camera : Panasonic Lumix G7 , Micro 4/3 D.M.C.
  • Lens : Vario 1:4.0-5.6 / 100-300mm power O.I.S.
  • Monopod : Joby ball head with smooth pan swivel

  • Capturing in RAW format , using high shutter speeds
    and low ISO for clean images .
    The mirrorless micro 4/3 sensor and 300mm lens gets up-close to the action.
    Knowing the best angels and positions to shot
    from guarantees great photos.
